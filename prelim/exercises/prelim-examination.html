  • Another important part of the exam focused on the relationship between text preprocessing and text representation. In my answer, I explained why text preprocessing should performed first before doing text representation, in this step where we clean and standardize raw data by removing noise, lowercasing, stopword removal, special character removal, and tokenization, which makes the input more consistent and easier to process. On the other hand, text representation transform cleaned data into numerical form or known as numerical representation using techniques One-Hot Encoding, Bag-of-Words, TF-IDF, and Word Embeddings, so it becomes easier for machine learning algorithms to understand and process it. Through this, I was able to demonstrate how these two crucial processes are connected.
